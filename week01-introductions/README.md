## Key Themes from the Readings

### HYBHY Introduction - Hierarchical Organizations
- Building productive relationships with line managers
- Target audience: working professionals (not aspiring managers)
- Differing agendas between employees and managers
- The reality of power dynamics in corporate settings

### ProducingOSS Introduction - Peer-Based Collaboration
- Voluntary contribution without formal authority
- Culture of choice rather than obligation
- Meritocracy: connection proportional to contribution
- Distributed collaboration without face-to-face meetings
